Experimenting with content design - Consumer Product Safety

Are enhancements to the landing page, compared to the existing one, resulting in more Canadians proceeding to the consumer incident report form?
Organization: Health Canada

Details Program: Consumer Product Safety Program
Summary of the Findings: Users who were sent to the modified landing page have accessed the report form in greater proportions than those sent to the existing page. The difference (34.0%) in the percentage of visitors who accessed the report form is statistically significant. This difference indicates that making the landing page more user-focused played an important role in encouraging users to access the incident report form.
Authority: Canada Consumer Product Safety Act
URL to Service: http://health.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/consumer-product-safety/advisories-warnings-recalls/report-incident-involving-consumer-product-b.html
Client/Target Groups:
  • Canadian Consumers, Industry
